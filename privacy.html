Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2025

1. Overview

This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our AI companion experience. We are committed to keeping your data safe and using it responsibly.

2. Information we collect

3. How we use information

We use collected information to operate the service, personalize experiences, improve performance, prevent abuse, and provide customer support. We may also use aggregated data to understand trends and improve the product.

4. Cookies and analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, measure performance, and improve the experience. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

5. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with trusted service providers for hosting, analytics, payment processing, and security, under strict confidentiality obligations.

6. Data retention

We keep data only as long as necessary to provide the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data. No system is 100% secure, so we encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your access details private.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. You can also request a copy of your information or object to certain processing activities. Contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email support@get-honey.today.
